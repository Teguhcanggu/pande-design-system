The small uppercase kicker that labels a section, prefixed with a coral tick. Pair it with an `<h2>` below.

```jsx
<Eyebrow>02 — Stay</Eyebrow>
<Eyebrow onDark>05 — Book</Eyebrow>
```

Use sienna by default; `onDark` switches to warm peach for earth/book surfaces. Often numbered to follow the Arrive → Stay → Belong → Explore → Book narrative.
