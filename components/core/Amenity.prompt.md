A single amenity in the "everything you need to settle in" band — icon, bold label, small note. Lay out in a 4-column grid on the Coconut Husk surface.

```jsx
<Amenity icon="❄️" label="Air conditioning" note="Cool, quiet rooms" />
<Amenity icon="🍳" label="Shared kitchen" note="Cook & meet travelers" />
```

The brand uses simple emoji glyphs for amenity and explore icons — keep that, don't swap in a heavy icon set.
