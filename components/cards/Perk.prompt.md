A direct-booking perk tile, designed for the dark earth "Book direct & save" band. Coral glyph, white serif title, muted body. Lay out four across.

```jsx
<Perk icon="◎" title="Best price, direct">Our lowest available rate, straight from us.</Perk>
<Perk icon="✆" title="Local host on WhatsApp">Real people, real replies — before & during.</Perk>
```

Uses simple geometric/unicode glyphs (◎ ✶ ✆ ↺) rather than an icon library. Only on dark surfaces — the translucent fill and light border need an earth background.
